Welcome! This is an overview of ten G20 countries (top 10 GDP income, excluding the European Union) over the years varying in range from 2010-2017.

The domains reviewed include military, healthcare, and education expenditures.

These are considered by currency expended in terms of total current US dollars, and USD and percent of spending by person, and gross domestic product (GDP) for each year.

  • Top GDP/person: United States
